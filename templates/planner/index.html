Hello, and welcome to Planner app

This app will help you to manage your budgets. You can keep your spending data from different bank accounts and your cash spending in one place.

You can create Categories and Budgets and make Transfers and Transactions with them.

If you forgot to make your transfer in moment of spending, don't worry date is editable here, just choose date and time you need.

You can have a negative balance in your budget which will represent your credits
